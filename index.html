Honda Civic Type R

The Honda Civic Type R is a high-performance variant of the Honda Civic, designed for driving enthusiasts who demand speed and precision. It is known for its aggressive styling and exceptional track performance.

Key Highlights

  • Engine: 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4, producing around 315 hp and 310 lb-ft of torque.
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual with rev-matching for smooth gear changes.
  • Performance: 0-60 mph in approximately 5.0 seconds, top speed of 170 mph.
  • Design: Functional air vents, large rear wing, wide fenders, and bold front styling.
  • Interior: Sport seats, red accents, and a driver-focused cockpit with performance telemetry.
  • Driving Dynamics: Front-wheel-drive with a limited-slip differential and Brembo brakes.

Technology & Safety

The Civic Type R comes with Honda Sensing, a suite of advanced safety features, and a digital dashboard designed to enhance the driving experience.
